About Mae Hornsby - Creator of Nigeria Travel Blog

Mae Hornsby

Software Engineer & Travel Enthusiast

Hello, I'm Mae Hornsby, a sophomore-year college student pursuing a degree in Software Engineering. I created Nigeria Travel Blog as part of my Object-Oriented Programming assignment, combining my passion for coding with my love for travel.

Through this blog, I aim to share my experiences exploring Nigeria and other African destinations, while also providing valuable insights and tips for fellow travelers. Join me on this exciting journey as we discover the beauty and diversity of Africa together!

At Nigeria Travel Blog, we are committed to promoting responsible and sustainable travel practices. We believe in supporting local communities and preserving the natural and cultural heritage of the places we visit.

Thank you for visiting Nigeria Travel Blog. I hope you find inspiration and useful information for your next adventure!

How I Created This Site

I built Nigeria Travel Blog as part of my journey in learning web development. I started by learning HTML and CSS from resources like W3Schools and Journi, where I gained fundamental knowledge of creating and styling web pages.

As I progressed, I delved into JavaScript, deepening my understanding through courses at Rochester University. Later on, I gained valuable experience at Webdroid Studios, where I worked for around 2 years. At Webdroid Studios, I honed my skills in graphic design, frontend development, and website hosting under the guidance of experienced professionals. This experience further promoted my understanding of software engineering and expanded my capabilities in design.
