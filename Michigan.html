Exploring the Mitten

Hometown Delicacies in Detroit

Hibachi

Enjoy traditional Japanese cuisine with dine-in and takeout options.

Coney Island

Experience the iconic Detroit coney dogs, a late-night favorite.

Upscale Dining

Indulge in fine dining experiences at upscale restaurants in Detroit, like Sexy Steak. This is a great new place for tourists and residents alike to try.

Popular Destinations in Michigan

Campus Martius, Downtown Detroit

Explore the vibrant heart of downtown Detroit with its bustling shops, restaurants, and cultural attractions.

GM Building, Detroit

Visit the iconic GM Building in downtown Detroit, a symbol of the city's automotive heritage and innovation.

Popular Activities in Michigan

Belle Isle

Explore the natural beauty and recreational activities of Belle Isle, a scenic island park located in the Detroit River.

Greenfield Village

Step back in time and experience life in 19th-century America at Greenfield Village, an open-air museum in Dearborn, Michigan.
