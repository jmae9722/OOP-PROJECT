Nigeria & General Travel Tips

Entry Requirements: A passport valid for six months, a visa, and proof of Polio and Yellow Fever vaccinations are required to enter Nigeria.

Local Laws and Regulations: Be aware of local laws and regulations; violating them may result in expulsion, arrest, or imprisonment.

Assistance for U.S. Citizens: If arrested or detained, ask police or prison officials to notify the U.S. Embassy or Consulate General immediately.

LGBTI Travelers: Consensual, same-sex sexual relations are illegal in Nigeria, with punishments including fines and prison sentences.

Religious Expression: Understand that many countries, including Nigeria, may restrict religious expression, including prayer, religious attire, and preaching.

Medical Facilities and Emergency Services: Nigerian medical facilities are generally poorly equipped, and many medicines are unavailable.

Michigan Travel Tips

Choose the best time for your trip to Michigan: The very best time to visit Michigan is September and October. September is like a second summer, and October has the best of the autumn weather. June through August are tremendously beautiful, but with kids out of school, you’ll be hard-pressed to find a campground to yourself, which isn’t a problem after Labor Day.

Winter in southern Michigan: Winter in southern Michigan is unpredictable and with climate change, leans toward dwindling. You will, though, absolutely be astonished by the snow in the UP.

Natural disasters: Michigan has one of the nation’s lowest rates of natural disasters.

Carhartt’s Workwear: Carhartt’s Workwear has also become a fashion statement across the state.

Puerto Rico Travel Tips

Traveling from the United States? No Passport Needed! Since Puerto Rico is a U.S. territory, American citizens and permanent residents can travel to the Island passport-free. All you need to bring is a valid ID, such as a driver's license or state ID card.

Chicago Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Chicago is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and many outdoor events take place.

Must-Visit Attractions: Don't miss the iconic Millennium Park, Navy Pier, the Art Institute of Chicago, and the Skydeck Chicago for stunning views of the city.
